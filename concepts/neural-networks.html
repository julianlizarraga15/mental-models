What layers do

Early layers

Detect simple signals: edges, words, prices, distances.

Middle layers

Combine simple signals into reusable patterns.

Late layers

Turn patterns into the task output: class, score, box, token.

Mental model

A neural network is a stack of learned transformations.

Each layer rewrites the data into a more useful representation. The last layer reads that representation and produces the prediction.
