IoU

Intersection over Union

IoU asks: how much of the two boxes is shared?

IoU = overlap area / total area covered by both boxes.

0 → 1

How to read it

0 means no overlap. 1 means perfect match. A common rule is IoU ≥ 0.5 counts as a correct detection.

⚠️

Important detail

High confidence is not enough. A box can be very confident and still be wrong if its IoU is too low.

For one label, AP summarizes the precision-recall tradeoff across confidence thresholds.

mAP

mAP is the mean of AP scores. It averages across classes, and sometimes across IoU thresholds too.

COCO

mAP@[.50:.95]

COCO-style mAP is stricter: it averages AP at IoU 0.50, 0.55, …, 0.95.
