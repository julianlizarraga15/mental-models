Concept

OCR

OCR turns text that appears inside an image or PDF into text data you can search, copy, and process.

Input
Scanned page, blueprint, receipt, table, or form.
↓
Find text regions
Locate the parts of the image that contain text.
↓
Read text
Convert the visual text into normal text.
↓
Output
Words, positions, lines, tables, and fields.

Simple mental model

OCR is a vision pipeline for reading. First it finds text visually. Then it converts that visual text into usable data.
